Exhibitor Guidelines

Everything you need to know to set up and run your stall smoothly.

Booth Setup

Setup begins one day prior to the event; all stalls must be ready two hours before opening.

Display Guidelines

Displays must stay within your allotted stall boundary and approved height limits.

Electricity Usage

Use only the sanctioned load for your package; report additional power needs in advance.

Safety Rules

Keep aisles clear, follow fire-exit signage, and avoid overloading electrical points.

Internet Facility

Complimentary Wi-Fi is provided; dedicated leased lines can be requested for demos.

Branding Policy

All branding must be approved by the organizing team before installation on-site.

Visitor Interaction

Keep at least one representative at your stall during all published show hours.

Waste Management

Segregate waste using the bins provided and avoid single-use plastic where possible.

Product Demonstration

Live demos are welcome; keep sound levels considerate of neighbouring stalls.

Photography Rules

Photography is permitted at your own stall; official media coverage is arranged separately.

Logistics

Loading and unloading is coordinated through designated bays during setup windows.

Emergency Support

An on-site help desk and medical team are available throughout the exhibition.
